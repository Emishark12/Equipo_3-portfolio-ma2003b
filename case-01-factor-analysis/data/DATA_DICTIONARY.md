# Data Dictionary - Customer Satisfaction Analysis

This document describes the variables contained in the `customer_satisfaction_data.csv` dataset.

## Input Variables (Features)

These variables represent different aspects of service quality and customer satisfaction, evaluated on a scale (e.g., 1-5 or 1-10).

### Technical Excellence & Innovation
*   `technical_expertise`: Level of technical expertise of the team.
*   `problem_solving`: Ability to solve problems effectively.
*   `innovation_solutions`: Proposition of innovative solutions.
*   `technical_documentation`: Quality and utility of the technical documentation provided.
*   `system_integration`: Effectiveness in system integration.

### Relationship Management & Communication
*   `account_manager_responsive`: Responsiveness and attention of the account manager.
*   `executive_access`: Ease of access to company executives.
*   `trust_reliability`: Level of trust and reliability in the provider.
*   `long_term_partnership`: Focus on building a long-term partnership.
*   `communication_clarity`: Clarity and effectiveness in communication.

### Project Delivery & Quality
*   `project_management`: Quality of project management.
*   `timeline_adherence`: Adherence to established deadlines.
*   `budget_control`: Ability to stay within budget.
*   `quality_deliverables`: General quality of project deliverables.
*   `change_management`: Effective handling of changes in scope or requirements.

### Value & Financial Transparency
*   `cost_transparency`: Clarity and transparency in costs and prices.
*   `value_for_money`: Perception of value received in relation to cost.
*   `roi_demonstration`: Ability to demonstrate return on investment (ROI).
*   `competitive_pricing`: Competitiveness of prices in the market.
*   `billing_accuracy`: Accuracy and correctness in billing.

### Support & Service Excellence
*   `support_responsiveness`: Speed and effectiveness of the support team.
*   `training_quality`: Quality of training offered to users.
*   `documentation_help`: Utility of help and support documentation.

## Outcome Variables (Outcomes)

These variables are key business metrics influenced by the factors above.

*   `overall_satisfaction`: Overall customer satisfaction score.
*   `nps_score`: Net Promoter Score (likelihood to recommend the service).
*   `renewal_likelihood`: Likelihood that the customer will renew the contract.
*   `revenue_growth_pct`: Percentage of customer revenue growth.
*   `referrals_generated`: Number of referrals or recommendations generated by the customer.
